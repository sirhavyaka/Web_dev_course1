Gojo Satoru
  • Born on 7th December, 1989, to the Gojo family of renowned Jujutsu Sorcerers.
  • First individual in 400 years to inherit the family's "Six-Eyes" (Rikugan) technique, which gives superhuman senses, and also the Limitless Cursed technique.
  • Widely renowned due to his talent and power, many had tried to hunt him down, but all of them were unsuccessful.
  • Toji Fushiguro from the renowned Zen'in family almost succeeded in killing him, but Gojo survived, using a reverse cursed technique to heal himself.
  • He was an influential teacher at Tokyo Jujutsu High.
  • He was one of the 4 Special-Grade sorcerers, who stand on the top of the Jujustu world.
  • His notable techniques were Infinity Void, Cursed-technique Lapse:Blue, Cursed-technique Reversal:Red and Hollow-technique:Purple.
  • He mentored talented students like Yuji Itadori, Megumi Fushiguro, Nobara Kugisaki, Yuta Okkotsu etc.
  • He was caught off-guard by Kenjaku and other curses in Shibuya, and was sealed in the Prison realm.
  • He was rescued by his colleagues and students, but Sukuna confronted him and both of them fought a long, hard battle.
  • However, Gojo was finally killed by Sukuna, who used Mahoraga to counter Gojo's techniques.

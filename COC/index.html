About us:

Animal Mafia is an active Clash of Clans clan community consisiting of five clans. Salient features of our clan community are:

  • Five clans active in donations, Clan Wars, Clan Games and Clan war league.
  • All the reward tiers are unlocked during Clan games every month.
  • Friendly and helpful members who donate and provide attack advice during clan wars.

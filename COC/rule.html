Community Rules:

  • Chat only in either English or Spanish.

  • Completing all the attacks in Weekly Clan Capital Raids is mandatory.
  • Enroll yourself in Clan Wars/Clan War Leagues only if you are sure you can get your attacks in. In case of last-second emergencies, inform the clan leader about it.
